Pinheiros Bravos

Alentejo, Portugal

The Property

Pinheiros Bravos is a secluded quinta set on 4.5 hectares of cork oaks and maritime pines in the rolling hills of the Alentejo, 10 km from the wild beaches of Melides and 75 minutes from Lisbon.

Designed by architect José Neves and completed in 2020, the house sits lightly in its landscape — three bedrooms, a large living and dining space that opens radically to the outdoors, and a rooftop hot tub looking out over nothing but forest canopy and sky.

The grounds have been lovingly shaped to include orchards, herb and flower gardens, a natural freshwater pond, and kilometres of walking trails winding through the estate and into the surrounding hills.

Comfortable year-round — radiant floor heating in winter, thick stone walls and shade in summer. High-speed Starlink, EV charging, and photovoltaic power with battery storage. The village of Melides (5 km) has groceries, restaurants, and access to several unspoiled Atlantic beaches. Cleaning and meal preparation available on request.
